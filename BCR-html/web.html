This paper focuses on the time-clustering analysis of sports videos. The core is to explore the clustering distribution of video releases under different time dimensions (weekday - hour and month - hour), as well as the scale and performance differences of clustering.

Cluster analysis: distribution of viewing behavior patterns

  1. Top left and top right: you can see exactly where the clusters are located.
  2. Lower left bar chart: although different “Time clustering” video posted by more or less people, but the interactive effect points are not much difference.
  3. Bottom - right pie chart: Cluster 2(Green) has the highest percentage (30.2%), indicating that this is the most popular type of “Temporal clustering” for videos, proving that it really works and has a large audience.

To get more people watching and interacting with sports videos, prioritize the first two charts to focus on clusters, especially Cluster 2, from Monday to Wednesday, and from afternoon to evening in summer and fall.

周期性模式分析：观看时间的周期性变化

Week

Wednesday, Thursday, the most popular weekend;

In a day

Afternoon to evening (12 - 18 o'clock) send the most people;

Within a year

July (summer) and the third quarter (july - september) are peak release seasons

Weekday distribution

Looking at the number of Weekday releases, you may find that the number of midweek (Tuesday - thursday) releases is relatively stable, while the number of Friday and Saturday releases is up, and the number of Sunday releases may be down slightly.

Hour distribution

At different hours of the day, the number of posts shows a clear periodicity. The number of posts usually reaches a peak between 6 pm and 10 pm, which is consistent with people's daily schedule. Evening is the main time period for people to relax and entertainment, and people pay more attention to sports videos. In the early morning, the number of posts is very small.

Month distribution

In the dimension of Month, the number of releases in some months is larger, which may be related to the arrangement of sports events and seasonal characteristics in that Month.

Quarter Distribution

On a quarterly basis, you may find a relatively high number of releases in Q2 and Q4.

标签优化

热门标签

#体育 #健身 #运动 #教学

使用相关性高的标签能提升视频的搜索排名。建议每个视频使用5-8个标签，包含主要关键词和长尾关键词。避免使用过于宽泛或与内容无关的标签。

描述优化

描述要点

  • 前50字包含关键信息
  • 包含相关关键词
  • 添加行动号召

视频描述是搜索引擎优化的重要因素。好的描述应该简洁明了，在前50个字符内概括视频核心内容，同时自然融入关键词，并在结尾加入订阅、点赞等行动号召。

标题优化

优质标题特征

长度控制在60字符内
包含数字和具体信息
使用情感词汇

标题是吸引观众点击的第一要素。有效的标题应该准确描述内容，激发好奇心，并包含关键词以提升搜索排名。避免使用夸大或误导性的表述，保持真实性。

时长分析

最佳时长区间

教学类 8-12分钟
娱乐类 3-6分钟
深度解析 15-20分钟

视频时长直接影响观看完成率和用户参与度。不同类型的内容有不同的最佳时长。关键是确保内容密度适中，避免冗长的开场和结尾，让每一分钟都有价值。

数据洞察

体育类视频在周末的观看量比工作日高出35%，建议在周五-周日发布重要内容。同时，视频时长控制在8-12分钟之间可获得最佳观看体验。
